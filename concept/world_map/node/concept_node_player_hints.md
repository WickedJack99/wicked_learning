## 2. Messages **from learners to learners**

Public or semi-public, placed at the beginning of a node.

Purpose:

> “What would someone who came before me tell me?”

This is the Dark Souls idea.

And I agree with your spoiler point. In this kind of platform, “spoiling” is not necessarily bad. Learning is not a purity test.

If someone writes:

> “Pay attention to the log timestamps here.”

That is not cheating. That is guidance.

Especially in a world with AI and internet access, trying to prevent all hints becomes weirdly controlling. Better to design around the assumption:

> Learners may use help, hints, notes and each other.

That fits SDT much better.
